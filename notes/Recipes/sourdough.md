# Weekend sourdough

A slow, forgiving loaf. Mostly waiting.

## Ingredients

| Item          | Amount  | Baker's % |
|---------------|---------|-----------|
| Bread flour   | 500 g   | 100%      |
| Water         | 350 g   | 70%       |
| Starter       | 100 g   | 20%       |
| Salt          | 10 g    | 2%        |

## Method

1. **Autolyse** — mix flour and water, rest 1 hour.
2. Add starter and salt, fold to combine.
3. **Bulk ferment** 4–5 h, folding every 30 min for the first 2 h.
4. Shape, then cold-proof overnight in the fridge.
5. Bake at `250°C` in a dutch oven — 20 min lid on, 20 min lid off.

> If the dough feels alive and jiggly before shaping, you're on track.
